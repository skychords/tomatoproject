Hypertext Markup Language (HTML) is the standard markup Language for creating web pages and web applications. today we announced the Beta of a group calendaring service
developed by the W3C Systems Team. The tool aims to help W3C Groups schedule their meetings and share them with the W3C community.

A new tab is now available from our group pages, lists all upcoming meetings a given group has scheduled, and enables in particular viewing times in different time zones (including your own), exporting and subscribing to an iCal feed.
